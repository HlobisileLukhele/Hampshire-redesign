Welcome to Hampshire

A warm welcome to the Coast

Hampshire Hotel Ballito was created for travellers who want the ease of a boutique stay with the comfort of a full-service hotel. Every space — from the rooms to the deck — is designed to feel calm, generous and unmistakably coastal.

Our team takes pride in the details: a genuine welcome, a quiet room, and a stay that feels effortless from the moment you arrive.
