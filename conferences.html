Conference Space

The perfect balance of professionalism and warmth

Discover expertly appointed rooms at Hampshire Hotel Ballito. Whether you're hosting a large-scale conference for up to 120 attendees or an intimate boardroom meeting, our two conference venues and one boardroom totaling over 1,737 sq ft provide a refined yet functional setting.

All spaces are equipped with essentials like whiteboards, flip-charts, high-speed internet, and modern communication infrastructure. Our dedicated on-site meetings coordinator ensures flawless service from start to finish.
