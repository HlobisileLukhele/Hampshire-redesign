On the Dolphin Coast

Moments from the beach, minutes from everything

Set in the heart of Ballito, Hampshire Hotel places you within easy reach of golden beaches, tidal pools and the Dolphin Coast's best restaurants — with King Shaka International a short drive away.

  • Walking distance to Willard & Salmon Bay beaches
  • 20 minutes from King Shaka International Airport
  • Close to Ballito Junction Regional Mall
